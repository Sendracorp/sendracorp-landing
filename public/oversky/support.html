Common questions

The Lock Screen weather isn't updating

iOS is conservative about waking apps in the background. The indicator updates every few hours when iOS allows. Open the app once to force an immediate refresh, or toggle "Show Weather on Lock Screen" off and on in OverSky's Settings.

I don't see the Dynamic Island indicator

The Dynamic Island only exists on iPhone 14 Pro and later Pro / Pro Max models. On other iPhones the indicator only appears on the Lock Screen, not in the status bar.

The temperature unit is wrong

Open OverSky → tap the gear icon → switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit under Display.

Location permission was denied

Without location, OverSky shows whatever was last cached or an error. Grant access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → OverSky → While Using the App. If you want the Lock Screen indicator, choose Always.

I'm seeing ads — can I remove them?

OverSky is free and ad-supported. A Pro subscription that removes ads (and adds a 7-day forecast) is planned for a future update.

Reporting a bug

Please include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version (Settings → General → About).
  • OverSky version (in-app Settings → About).
  • What you did, what you expected, and what actually happened.
  • A screenshot if possible.

We typically reply within a couple of business days.
