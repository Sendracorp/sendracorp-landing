Privacy Policy

OverSky for iOS
Effective date: 2026-04-15. Last updated: 2026-04-15.

Sendracorp ("we", "us", "our") operates the OverSky mobile application (the "App"). This page tells you what data the App collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. Plain language, no surprises.

Information we collect

Location. When you grant permission, the App requests your device's approximate location from iOS in order to fetch tomorrow's weather forecast for where you are. Your location is sent directly to Open-Meteo (our weather data provider) as part of a single API request. The coordinate is also stored locally on your device so the App can show a forecast when you're temporarily offline. We do not transmit your location to our own servers — we don't run any.

If you enable the optional "Show Weather on Lock Screen" feature, the App requests "Always" location permission so it can refresh the forecast in the background a few times per day. The same data flow applies — location goes to Open-Meteo for the fetch, then is discarded.

Advertising identifiers. The App displays a single banner ad served by Google AdMob. If you grant the App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob may access your device's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to deliver more relevant ads. If you decline, AdMob still serves ads, but they are non-personalized. Either choice is fine — the App works the same way.

What we do not collect. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or any account information. The App has no login. We do not run analytics or crash-reporting SDKs. We have no servers and no database.

How we use this data

  • Your location is used solely to fetch your local weather forecast.
  • Advertising identifiers, when authorized, are used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties beyond the two service providers named below.

Third-party services

Open-Meteo — our weather data provider. Receives the coordinate of each forecast request. See Open-Meteo's terms.

Google AdMob — serves the banner ad in the App. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may, with your ATT consent, use your IDFA and limited usage signals for ad personalization and measurement.

Your choices

  • Location. You can revoke location permission anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → OverSky. The App will continue to work using a previously cached forecast when location is unavailable.
  • Tracking. You can change your ATT decision anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → OverSky. AdMob will switch between personalized and non-personalized ads accordingly.
  • Lock Screen indicator. Toggle off inside the App's Settings to disable background updates and stop "Always" location use.

Children

OverSky is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App has no accounts, messaging, or user-generated content.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
