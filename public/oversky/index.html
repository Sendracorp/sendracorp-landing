OverSky

Tomorrow's weather, glanceable

About

OverSky is a native iOS weather app that shows tomorrow's forecast for your current location, with an optional persistent indicator on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Free, ad-supported. Available on the App Store.

Privacy

OverSky takes privacy seriously. We use your location only to show your local weather, and we don't sell or share your data.
