About

Sendracorp is an independent software studio founded by Pol Sendra. We build a small number of products at a time, across native apps, games, and blockchain — and ship them ourselves.

Our background spans smart-contract development at Aave and BGD Labs, blockchain-based credit infrastructure at Raise, full-stack real-estate tooling at AgentSelly, and conversational commerce at correYvuela. That history shapes how we work today: opinionated about quality, comfortable across the stack, and unafraid of the systems-level details — from Solidity to Swift.

What we build

Apps

Native iOS applications built with Swift and SwiftUI. We care about platform conventions, performance, and the small details that make software feel finished.

Games

Small, self-contained games with a single tight idea — designed to be picked up in a minute and remembered for longer.

On-chain

Smart contracts, protocol tooling, and Web3 integrations — drawing on production experience in DeFi and Ethereum-based systems.
